Daily Checklist

Simple, repeatable checks for a healthy system.

Health checks

  1. Open /status/ and verify ws is active.
  2. Open /canvas/ and confirm nodes render.
  3. Open /gltf-live/ and confirm the preview animates.
  4. Open /wiki/ and verify the Welcome entry renders.

Update the bundle

  1. Regenerate assets (if needed).
  2. Rebuild the tarball: tetragrammatron/public/releases/.
  3. Verify /install.sh still runs.

Backup

  • Archive /home/main/tetragrammatron/public/releases/.
  • Back up event logs in /home/main/tetragrammatron/core/virtual-revelation/data/.
